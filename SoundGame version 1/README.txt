This zipped folder is the Assets folder of the first version of Soun Box.  There is one script
called Soundstuff.cs that does all the heavy lifting of this program and it is a Component of 
a Sphere in the game world.

Controls:

A - move Sphere away from camera
S - move Sphere towards camera
X - increase pitch of song
C - decrease pitch of song
U - switchs songs
Q - play song
W - pause song
B - increase volume
V - decrease volume
N - increase spread angle
M - decrease spread angle
R - randomly pick rolloff mode
